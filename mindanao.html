    Communal Ranch

    Nestled amidst the rolling hills of Impasugong, Bukidnon in Mindanao, Philippines, lies the Communal Ranch. This scenic destination offers a glimpse into a unique agricultural setting, known for its breathtaking mountain vistas and refreshing atmosphere.


    Location/Jump-off: Impasugong, Bukidnon, Mindanao, Philippines. Accessible via various routes depending on your starting point.
    Contact: Information on a specific contact might be limited due to the nature of the ranch. It's advisable to reach out to local tourism offices in Impasugong or Bukidnon for guidance.
    Best Things to Do: Enjoy panoramic mountain views, experience a working ranch environment (depending on accessibility), explore the surrounding nature, and take in the crisp mountain air.

    Dahilayan Adventure Park

    Craving an adrenaline rush amidst a cool, mountainous landscape? Look no further than Dahilayan Adventure Park, the ultimate playground for thrill-seekers in Mindanao, Philippines. Nestled in Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon, this park boasts breathtaking scenery and a heart-pounding selection of extreme activities.


    Location/Jump-off: Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon, Philippines
    Contact: (0917) 622 3204 [link to Dahilayan Adventure Park website]
    Best Things to Do: Conquer your fears and soar through the lush canopy on Asia's longest dual zipline (840 meters!), freefall into a man-made lake on the Dropzone, experience the thrill of the Skytower Base Jump, race down the slopes on the Razorback Mountain Coaster, and challenge yourself on the 12-stage ropes course.

    Enchanted River

    The Enchanted River, also known as the Hinatuan Enchanted River or Hinatuan Sacred River, is a natural wonder nestled in the lush jungles of Hinatuan, Surigao del Sur, Mindanao, Philippines. This mesmerizing river boasts crystal-clear, sapphire-hued waters with exceptional clarity, extending for over 80 feet and flowing directly into the Philippine Sea. Local legends add to the mystique, attributing the vibrant color and unknown depths to fairies or mysterious fish.


    Location/Jump-off: Barangay Talisay, Hinatuan, Surigao del Sur, Mindanao, Philippines
    Contact: Hinatuan Municipal Tourism Office: (+63) 86-213-2021
    Best Things to Do: Swimming (designated area), kayaking, boat tours, exploring the surrounding rainforest, and marveling at the breathtaking natural beauty.

    Samal Island

    Escape to paradise at Samal Island, also known as the Island Garden City of Samal, in Davao del Norte, Mindanao, Philippines. This gem boasts pristine beaches, lush hills, and crystal-clear waters, making it a haven for relaxation and adventure.


    Location/Jump-off: Island Garden City of Samal, Davao del Norte, Mindanao, Philippines. Ferries depart from Davao City.
    Contact: Davao del Norte Tourism Office: (+63) 82 227-2297
    Best Things to Do: Beach lounging, diving, snorkeling, exploring coral reefs, visiting the Giant Clam Sanctuary, trekking through rainforests, spelunking in Hagimit Falls, experiencing water sports like jetskiing and parasailing, indulging in fresh seafood, and soaking up the laid-back island vibes.

    Zamboanga City

    Nicknamed the "City of Flowers," Zamboanga City on the southwestern tip of the Zamboanga Peninsula in Mindanao, Philippines, is a vibrant hub known for its colorful festivals, rich cultural heritage, and stunning natural beauty. Nestled between the Zamboanga Peninsula and the Sulu Sea, the city boasts a strategic location, historical significance, and a unique blend of Spanish, Chavacano, Tausug, and Filipino influences.


    Location/Getting There: Zamboanga City, Zamboanga Peninsula, Mindanao, Philippines. Accessible by air through Zamboanga International Airport (ZAM) and by sea through Zamboanga City Ferry Terminal.
    Contact: Zamboanga City Tourism Office: (+63) 62-981-1094
    Best Things to Do: Explore Fort Pilar, a 17th-century Spanish fort; immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of the Zamboanga City Hall; witness the colorful spectacle of the Hermosa Festival; and savor the city's delectable cuisine.
