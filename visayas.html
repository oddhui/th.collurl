    Kawasan Falls

    Nestled amidst the lush jungles of Badian, Cebu in the Visayas region of the Philippines, Kawasan Falls is a cascading marvel. This three-tiered waterfall boasts crystal-clear turquoise water cascading over limestone formations, creating a picture-perfect natural pool ideal for swimming. Lush greenery surrounds the falls, offering a refreshing escape and a true communion with nature.


    Location/Jump-off: Barangay Matutinao, Badian, Cebu, Philippines
    Contact: Badian Tourism Office: (+63) 32-471-2001
    Best Things to Do: Take a refreshing dip in the natural pool, go canyoneering for an adrenaline rush, explore the various tiers of the falls, have a picnic lunch by the river, and witness the breathtaking beauty of the surrounding jungle.

    Kalanggaman Island

    Escape to paradise at Kalanggaman Island, a sandbar island nestled in the turquoise waters between Leyte and Cebu in the Visayas region of the Philippines. Renowned for its powdery white sand beach and crystal-clear waters, Kalanggaman Island offers a tranquil retreat for relaxation and exploration.


    Location/Jump-off: Palompon, Leyte, Philippines (ferry or boat ride)
    Contact: Local tourism authorities in Palompon can provide information on travel arrangements and accredited boat operators.
    Best Things to Do: Swimming, sunbathing, snorkeling, sandcastle building, beach volleyball, camping (with permission), enjoying fresh seafood, and soaking up the breathtaking scenery.

    Hinagdanan Cave

    Escape the heat and discover a hidden gem in Bohol Island, Visayas, Philippines! Hinagdanan Cave, which translates to "laddered cave" in Cebuano, is a naturally lit cavern with a fascinating backstory. Legend says a local farmer accidentally discovered it while clearing his land and used a ladder ("hagdan") to access its wonders within.


    Location/Jump-off: Dauis Municipality, Panglao Island, Bohol Province, Philippines
    Contact: Barangay Dauis Tourism Office: (+63) 38-485-2001 (Possible contact, subject to change)
    Best Things to Do: Marvel at the natural rock formations (stalactites & stalagmites), take a refreshing dip in the cool, deep lagoon (entrance fee applies for swimming), explore the cave with natural light filtering through, and capture unique photos in this picturesque location.

    Boracay Island

    Boracay Island, a jewel of the Visayas region in the Philippines, is a world-renowned paradise known for its powdery white sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant atmosphere. This postcard-perfect island boasts a 4.7-kilometer stretch of White Beach, consistently ranked among the best beaches globally. Beyond the stunning beaches, Boracay offers exciting water activities, vibrant nightlife, and a diverse range of restaurants catering to all tastes.


    Location/Jump-off: Caticlan Airport, Malay, Aklan, Philippines (requires ferry transfer to Boracay Island)
    Contact: Aklan Tourism Office: (+63) 36-268-6201
    Best Things to Do: Relaxing on the beach, swimming, snorkeling, diving, helmet diving, island hopping, spelunking, cliff diving, windsurfing, kitesurfing, paragliding, exploring local markets, enjoying delicious seafood, and experiencing the lively nightlife.

    Chocolate Hills

    The Chocolate Hills, a geological marvel in Carmen, Bohol, Visayas, Philippines, are a captivating landscape unlike any other. These cone-shaped hills, numbering over 1,200, rise majestically from the surrounding plains. During the dry season, their verdant grass cover transforms into a rich chocolate brown, giving rise to their delightful name. This unique phenomenon continues to baffle geologists and enthrall visitors.


    Location/Jump-off: Carmen, Bohol, Philippines
    Contact: Bohol Tourism Office: (+63) 38-501-2744
    Best Things to Do: Witness the panoramic view from Chocolate Hills Complex viewing deck, explore the surrounding hills by motorbike or bicycle, go spelunking in nearby caves, learn about the fascinating geological history at the on-site museum (if available), and capture stunning photos of the unique landscape.
